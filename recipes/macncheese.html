Mac and Cheese

Return to main page

Description

Pasta and cheese. Very tasty very yum.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter and flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Seasonings
  • Bread Crumbs

Steps

  1. Boil macaroni and drain the water. Put into baking tray.
  2. Make the cheese sauce, pour the sauce over the noodles, and stir.
  3. Make the topping, spread it over macaroni and cheese, and sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Bake the mac and cheese until the topping is golden brown.
